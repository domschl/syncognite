◆ USE_FLOAT

#define USE_FLOAT

Define either USE_FLOAT (default) or USE_DOUBLE to determine the precision of the matrix and math operations. All other main types (like MatrixN or VectorN ) are derrived from those definitions

MatrixN is the standard type for matrices in syncognite. It's either float or double, depending on the definition of USE_FLOAT or USE_DOUBLE
